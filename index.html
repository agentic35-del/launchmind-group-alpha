How it works

SkillSync keeps the workflow simple: analyze the posting, parse your resume, compare the signals, and show a concise report you can act on immediately.

1

Detect the active job posting

SkillSync reads the posting in your browser and extracts required skills, tools, seniority clues, and key responsibilities.

2

Upload or paste your resume

Add a PDF resume or text version so SkillSync can identify skills, experience signals, and relevant evidence.

3

Get a gap report in seconds

See matched, partially matched, and missing skills, plus a prioritized next skill recommendation tied to the role.

Core features

Built for fast decisions, practical guidance, and a clean in-browser report that makes fit easy to understand.

Job Posting Skill Extraction

Automatically detects the current posting and extracts required skills, tools, experience level, and responsibilities.

Resume Upload and Parsing

Supports resume PDF upload or pasted text to identify skills, tools, and experience signals from your background.

Skills Gap Comparison

Compares job requirements with your resume to classify items as matched, partially matched, or missing.

Personalized Gap Report Panel

Shows a simple fit summary inside the browser so you can understand your alignment in under 30 seconds.

Next Skill Recommendation

Recommends the single most important skill to learn next, with a reason tied directly to the role requirements.

Evidence Highlighting

Displays the resume phrases that matched each requirement so you can see why skills were covered or flagged.

Target users

SkillSync is designed for different stages of a tech career, from first applications to senior role targeting.

Fresh graduate

Data job seeker

A recent graduate applying to entry-level data analyst, BI analyst, or junior data scientist roles.

  • Needs to translate resume content into job requirements
  • Wants to know which keywords and tools to emphasize
Career pivot

Transitioning into data science

A professional moving from another field into data science and mapping prior experience to technical postings.

  • Needs to identify the most important skill gaps
  • Wants clear prioritization on what to learn next
Mid-level to senior

ML engineer targeting senior roles

An ML engineer comparing current experience against advanced role requirements around leadership, tooling, and deployment.

  • Needs fast, precise gap analysis
  • Wants to focus on realistic opportunities
