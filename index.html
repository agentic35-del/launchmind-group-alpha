How it works

SkillSync keeps the workflow simple: capture the job posting, parse your resume, compare the two, and surface the most useful next step.

1

Open a job posting

The extension reads the active listing in your browser and extracts required skills, preferred skills, responsibilities, seniority signals, and domain keywords.

2

Import your resume

Upload or paste your resume once, then convert experience, projects, tools, and evidence into a structured profile SkillSync can compare.

3

Get skill-by-skill matching

Each requirement is classified as matched, partially matched, or missing, with evidence linked to the most relevant resume sections when available.

4

See what to do next

Receive a concise fit summary, ranked gaps, and a next-best skill recommendation tailored to the specific role and your current background.
