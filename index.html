SkillSync

Helps job seekers identify and close skills gaps by monitoring real-time job postings and comparing required skills with their resumes, providing actionable insights to improve employability.

Real-time Job Posting Monitoring & Skills-Gap Analysis

Key Features

Job Posting Monitoring

Continuously monitors job listings on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor while you browse to identify relevant job postings.

Skill Extraction

Automatically extracts required skills and qualifications from job postings using advanced NLP techniques.

Resume Upload & Parsing

Upload your resume to let SkillSync parse and identify your existing skills for accurate comparison.

Personalized Skills-Gap Report

Get detailed reports highlighting missing skills required for your desired jobs with actionable recommendations.

Interactive User Dashboard

Track monitored job postings, extracted skills, and past skills-gap reports all in one intuitive dashboard.

Notification Alerts

Receive timely alerts for new job postings and skill-gap updates relevant to your career goals.
