Features

Everything you need to identify skill gaps, prioritize improvements, and turn job descriptions into a clear action plan.

P0

Job Posting Detection

Automatically recognizes postings on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor, then extracts title, company, location, and description.

P0

Resume Upload and Import

Upload a PDF or DOCX once and parse your resume into structured skills, experience, and education fields.

P0

Skill Gap Matching Logic

Compares job requirements to resume-derived skills and scores matched, partially matched, and missing qualifications.

P0

Skill Extraction Engine

Uses rules and NLP to normalize hard skills, tools, certifications, and soft skills into a common taxonomy.

P0

Personalized Gap Report

Shows what matters most for each role, including evidence of related experience and the highest-impact gaps to close.

P1

Action Recommendations

Suggests resume keywords, portfolio examples, certifications, and learning resources based on top priorities.
