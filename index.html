Built for job seekers, active applicants, and resume coaches

🧭

Automatic job-post detection

Recognizes LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor pages in-browser and extracts title, company, location, and description text without copy-paste.

📄

Resume upload and import

Upload a resume PDF or DOCX once, then let SkillSync parse skills, experience, and education into a reusable profile.

🧠

Skill extraction and normalization

Uses rules and NLP to identify hard skills, tools, certifications, and soft skills, then deduplicates synonyms into a shared taxonomy.

Gap matching logic

Compares posting requirements against your resume, highlighting matched, partially matched, and missing skills by relevance.

📈

Personalized gap report

Generates a concise, prioritized report with evidence from your background and specific areas to learn or rephrase.

✨

Action recommendations

Suggests resume keyword updates, portfolio examples, certification ideas, and targeted learning resources for the biggest gaps.

How it works

A simple flow designed for real job-search behavior: browse jobs, detect the posting, compare against your resume, and act on the findings.

1

Install the extension

Add SkillSync to your browser and pin it for quick access while browsing job boards.

2

Upload your resume

Import a resume PDF or DOCX once so SkillSync can build a structured profile of your skills and experience.

3

Open a job posting

When you land on a posting in LinkedIn, Indeed, or Glassdoor, SkillSync detects it automatically and begins analysis.

4

Review and act

See missing skills, evidence from your resume, and next-step recommendations you can use immediately.
