How it works

A simple flow designed for speed: detect the posting, parse your resume, compare skills, then render a useful report instantly.

1

Detect the posting

SkillSync recognizes job pages on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor and scrapes the posting details automatically.

2

Import your resume

Upload your resume once and convert it into structured data the extension can compare against future postings.

3

Compare skills

The system normalizes and deduplicates skills, then scores matched, partial, and missing items by relevance.

4

Act on the report

Review a prioritized gap report with targeted recommendations to update your resume and strengthen candidacy.
