Features

Built for job seekers, active applicants, and career coaches who need a fast, repeatable way to compare a resume against a role and identify what matters most.

Auto Job Detection

Recognizes supported job pages on LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor and extracts title, company, location, and description without copy-paste.

Resume Import

Uploads PDF or DOCX resumes, then parses skills, experience, and education into structured data for analysis.

Skills Gap Analysis

Normalizes terms, deduplicates synonyms, and compares posting requirements against your resume to identify matched and missing skills.

Personalized Report

Generates a concise report with evidence of related experience, confidence scoring, and prioritized gaps for each posting.

Actionable Recommendations

Suggests resume keyword updates, portfolio proof points, certification ideas, and targeted learning resources.

Session History

Stores past analyses so users can revisit reports, track progress, and see repeated gaps across applications.

How it works

The workflow is simple: detect the posting, understand your resume, compare the requirements, and show exactly what to improve next.

1

SkillSync automatically identifies supported job pages and captures the listing details.

2

3

Compare

The engine extracts required skills and compares them to your resume with gap scoring.

4

Act

Get a prioritized report with recommendations you can use to tailor applications faster.
