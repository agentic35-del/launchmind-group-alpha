Features section

The core extension flow is designed around instant detection, structured resume intake, skill normalization, and a report that tells users exactly what to do next.

Job Posting Detection

Automatically detects supported job pages and extracts title, company, location, and description text.

Resume Upload and Parsing

Imports a resume once, converts it into structured skills and experience fields, and reuses it across postings.

Skills Matching Engine

Compares requirements to your background, deduplicates synonyms, and ranks the most important gaps.

Personalized Gap Report

Shows missing skills, partial matches, and evidence from your resume in a concise panel.

Action Recommendations

Suggests resume keyword updates, portfolio examples, certifications, and targeted learning resources.

Session History

Stores analyzed postings so users can revisit reports and track recurring gaps over time.

How it works

Three simple steps power the extension workflow from first visit to actionable insights.

1

Open a job posting

SkillSync detects LinkedIn, Indeed, or Glassdoor pages and extracts the job content automatically.

2

Upload your resume once

Import PDF or DOCX and let SkillSync parse your skills, experience, and education into a structured profile.

3

Review your gap report

See missing skills, matching evidence, and prioritized recommendations to improve your candidacy fast.
