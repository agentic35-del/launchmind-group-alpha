How it works

SkillSync is designed to fit naturally into the way people already browse jobs online.

1. Detect the job posting

The extension automatically recognizes a job description page and extracts responsibilities, required skills, preferred skills, tools, qualifications, and seniority signals from the visible content.

2. Parse your resume once

Upload or paste your resume and SkillSync turns education, experience, tools, keywords, and skills into a structured profile for fast comparison.

3. Get a skills-gap report

See what matches, what is missing, and what matters most — then receive next-step recommendations tailored to the specific role and your background.
